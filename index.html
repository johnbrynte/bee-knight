Pollinator

How to play

The world is in crisis. Bees are nowhere to be seen. Fortunately, Polly owns a farm where they seems to feel at home. She must hurry to pollinate here berry plants before the bees run away.

Controls

[Arrow keys] / [WASD]Walk
[Tab]Open bag / Put item in bag
[Space]Grab item (hold) / Drop item / Select item

Items

Bee hive: Where the bees stay.
Basket: Put your fruits here.
Flower: Pay attention to the species.
Berry: Put these in your basket!
